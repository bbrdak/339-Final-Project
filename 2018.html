THE CONCEPT

In 2018, the Boston Crusaders Performed the Show "S.O.S.", loosely based on the William Golding novel Lord of The Flies. The show follows the story of a group of travelers stranded on an island, forming alliances, dealing with conflict, and eventually being rescued. It features music including "Salvation Is Created" by Pavel Chesnokov, "Marimba Spiritual" by Minoru Miki, "End Credits (from Cast Away)" by Alan Silvestri, and more.

COMPETITIVE SUCCESS

The corps entered the 2018 season with the aim of improving upon the success of the year prior. From 2016 to 2017, the corps climbed from 12th place to 6th, and they entered 2018 hoping to build on this momentum. Over the course of the 2018 season, the Corps climbed all the way to 5th place overall at DCI finals, with a score of 94.3125. Although they only moved one placement, the season was still considered to be very successful. The corps also received the George Zingali award for best colorguard for the first time.
